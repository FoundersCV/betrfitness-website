How It Works

BetrFitness turns fitness consistency into a structured loop you can repeat and improve.

1. Set your baseline and goals

Start with your current training rhythm and define outcomes you want to hit, so the platform can build around your real routine.

2. Train with accountability loops

Use streaks, milestones, and progress checkpoints to stay engaged and reduce drop-off.

3. Measure progress that matters

Track completion trends and performance movement over time so effort translates into visible momentum.

4. Keep improving

As your consistency improves, adjust goals and challenge levels to keep your training purposeful and sustainable.
